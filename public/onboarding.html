Here's a quick look at what Zoutty does before you install.

Organize by student
Create a folder for a student, a class, or a topic. Sessions live inside.
Capture audio notes
Record notes, corrections, and key moments live during lessons.
AI session report
Tap the wand — get a structured report with key points, drills, and homework.
Cloud sync
Your sessions are backed up automatically. Nothing gets lost.
How it works
1
Create a folder for a student, a class, or a topic.
2
Open a session and tap the mic to capture notes during the lesson.
3
Tap the wand to generate an AI report from your notes.

2
