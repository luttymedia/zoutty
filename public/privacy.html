Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

We know privacy policies can be a pain to read, so we've kept this one as plain and short as possible. Here's exactly what Zoutty collects, how we use it, and your rights.

Who We Are

Zoutty is a private lesson companion app that helps teachers and students capture, organize, and revisit their lessons using audio notes and AI-generated reports. We are an independent developer project, not a large corporation.

What We Collect

  • Account information. If you create an account or sign in with Google, we store your email address and a unique user ID. We do not store your Google password.
  • Audio recordings. Audio clips you record during a session are stored on your device and, if you are signed in, uploaded to our secure cloud storage (Supabase) so you can access them across devices.
  • Session data. Session titles, notes, AI-generated reports, folders, and any media (photos or videos) you attach to a session.
  • Device data. Basic technical information (browser type, device type) that is automatically sent with any network request. We do not use this to track you.

Guest mode: If you use Zoutty as a guest, nothing is sent to our servers. All data stays on your device only.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the service. Your sessions, audio, and reports are stored so you can access and manage them.
  • To generate AI reports. When you tap the wand, your audio recordings are sent to the Google Gemini API for transcription and report generation. Google processes this audio according to their own privacy policy. We do not store the raw audio on Google's systems beyond what is needed to generate the report.
  • To sync your data. If you are signed in, your data is synced across devices using Supabase, a secure cloud backend.
  • We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not use your recordings or notes for advertising.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API — used to transcribe audio and generate session reports. Google Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — used for authentication, database storage, and file storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (OAuth) — optional login method. Your Google account is only used to verify your identity.
  • Render — used to host the Zoutty web application.

Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all your cloud data (sessions, audio, reports) is permanently deleted immediately.

If you use Zoutty as a guest, data is stored only in your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser data or switching devices will erase it.

Your Rights

  • Access. You can export all your data from the app settings at any time.
  • Deletion. You can delete individual sessions or your entire account from within the app.
  • Portability. Your data can be exported in a portable format from the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Zoutty is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Zoutty after changes means you accept the updated policy.
